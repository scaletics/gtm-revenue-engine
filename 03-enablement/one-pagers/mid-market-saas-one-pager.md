## Headline

Align revenue strategy, sales process, and enablement to improve pipeline conversion.

---

## The Problem

Many mid-market SaaS companies have activity, tools, and pipeline, but revenue performance is still constrained by inconsistent messaging, uneven qualification, unclear stage discipline, weak stakeholder alignment, and proposals that go out before the business case is strong enough.

The result is stalled pipeline, long sales cycles, inconsistent forecasts, and deals lost to no decision.

---

## Why It Matters

When the revenue system is misaligned:

- Reps qualify inconsistently.
- Champions struggle to sell internally.
- Forecasts become subjective.
- Buyers do not see enough urgency.
- Sales and marketing operate from different narratives.
- Proposals become documents instead of decision tools.

---

## Recommended Solution

Build a revenue infrastructure layer that connects ICP, messaging, sales process, enablement, and business-case development.

Core workstreams:

- Refine ICP and segmentation.
- Align positioning and value messaging.
- Define sales stages and exit criteria.
- Improve discovery and qualification discipline.
- Build stakeholder and business-case tools.
- Standardize proposal and follow-up assets.
- Create a repeatable pipeline review cadence.

---

## Expected Outcomes

- Clearer segment focus
- Stronger sales consistency
- Better buyer qualification
- Improved stakeholder alignment
- Fewer stalled opportunities
- Better proposal discipline
- Stronger forecast visibility
- More repeatable revenue execution

---

## Best Fit

This is best for SaaS companies that are:

- $2M to $50M ARR
- Sales-led or hybrid GTM
- Facing long or inconsistent sales cycles
- Selling through multiple stakeholders
- Experiencing stalled pipeline
- Preparing to enter a new market or segment
- Trying to improve conversion, velocity, or forecast quality

---
